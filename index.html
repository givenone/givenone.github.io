Junwon Seo

I am a robotics researcher at the AI Autonomy Technology Center of the Agency for Defense Development (ADD) in Republic of Korea. My primary research interest lies in the area of robotics and computer vision, particularly robot perception that enables uncertainty-aware navigation in complex and unknown environments. I completed my bachelor's degree (Summa Cum Laude) in Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University (SNU).

Email  |  CV  |  GitHub  |  Google Scholar  |  LinkedIn

Research Interests

I am interested in 3D/4D virtual humans. My goal is to realize an virtual world communication through efficient and realistic virtual humans. Currently, I'm focusing on below topics, but not limited to.

  • Accurate 3D pose and shape reconstruction of humans and animals.
  • Multi-modal 3D content generation for virtual world.
  • Human body texture reconstruction with limited visual cues.
